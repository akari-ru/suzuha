
Kara Specification v 0.1

Kara is an experimental language, whos sole purpose ist to learn, test and experiment with parsing for the infamous dev behind it.
We will define the language mathematical and try to include custom concepts as the infamous dev is not bound to normal human limits.


 Parts
*-----*

chars:
atomar particles with different meanings

words:
strings of chars seperated through a special seperator char - whitespace ' ' (continous whitespace while always collapse to a single seperator)

grouping aka scoping - brackets for this purpose:
( ), { }, [ ], < >

numbers - at first all ist integer 64bit:
0-9*

alphabethical chars:
A-Z, a-z
